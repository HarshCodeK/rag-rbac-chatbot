Q3 2025 Marketing Budget Breakdown

Total marketing spend for Q3 was $2.4 million. Digital advertising accounted for $980,000 with Google Ads at $520,000 and LinkedIn Ads at $310,000 and Meta Ads at $150,000. Content marketing and SEO efforts totaled $420,000 including blog production and freelance writers. Events and conferences cost $380,000 with our biggest expense being the annual industry summit at $180,000. Sales enablement tools and CRM integrations came to $240,000. Brand awareness campaigns including PR and thought leadership cost $210,000. Email marketing platforms and automation tools were $95,000. The remaining $75,000 was allocated to analytics tools and agency fees. Marketing qualified leads increased 22% quarter over quarter with a cost per lead of $185. The marketing team has requested an additional $300,000 for Q4 holiday campaigns.
