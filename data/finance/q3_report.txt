Q3 2025 Financial Report

Revenue for Q3 reached $24.7 million, representing 18% year-over-year growth. Gross profit margin improved to 72% compared to 68% in Q3 of last year. Operating expenses totaled $8.2 million, up 6% from the previous quarter primarily due to increased R&D investment. Net income came in at $4.1 million, beating our internal forecast by 12%. The customer acquisition cost decreased to $320 per new account, down from $380 in Q2. Annual recurring revenue (ARR) hit $98 million as of September 30. The board has approved a $2 million budget increase for the AI division in Q4. The finance team expects full-year revenue to exceed $95 million based on current pipeline strength. Department spending for Q3 saw Engineering at $3.1M, Marketing at $2.4M, Sales at $1.8M, and Operations at $0.9M.
