Payroll Policy — Acme Corp

Payroll is processed bi-weekly every other Friday. Direct deposit is mandatory and payroll deductions include federal and state income tax, Social Security, and Medicare. Employees may enroll in the 401(k) plan starting the first of the month after 60 days of employment. The company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary. Health insurance options include PPO and HSA-eligible high-deductible plans with premiums deducted pre-tax. Dental and vision coverage are available at group rates. Commuter benefits allow up to $300 per month pre-tax for transit and parking. Overtime is paid at 1.5x the hourly rate for non-exempt employees and must be pre-approved by the direct manager. Bonus payments are processed in the first payroll of February and August each year.
