Employee Handbook — Acme Corp

Paid Time Off: Full-time employees accrue 15 PTO days per year, increasing to 20 days after 3 years of service. Sick leave is separate and provides 10 paid sick days annually. Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal. Remote Work Policy: Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off and are reviewed quarterly. Code of Conduct: All employees must complete the annual ethics training by January 31. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed in writing to HR. Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination. Dress code is business casual in the office. Personal devices used for work must have company-approved security software installed.
