Acme Corp Company Overview

Acme Corp was founded in 2014 by Sarah Chen and Michael Torres with a mission to simplify enterprise data analytics through AI-powered tools. The company is headquartered in Austin, Texas with satellite offices in San Francisco and New York. Our core product suite includes AcmeAnalytics (business intelligence platform), AcmeFlow (workflow automation), and AcmeInsight (AI-driven recommendation engine). We serve over 2,000 enterprise customers across North America and Europe. Our company values are Customer First, Bias for Action, Radical Transparency, and Continuous Learning. Acme Corp has raised $120 million in Series C funding led by VenturePoint Capital. The current headcount is approximately 850 employees across engineering, sales, marketing, HR, and operations departments. Our fiscal year runs from January 1 to December 31 with quarterly board reviews.
